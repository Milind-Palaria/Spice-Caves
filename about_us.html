Spice   Caves

Serving since 1990 with love. Spice Caves' opened on Thanksgiving Day 1990. Chef / Owner Milind Palaria began baking pies and selling them to restaurants and his neighbors out of a small kitchen at the corner of Hudson and North Moore St. in Lucknow. U.P.'s beloved restaurant and pie shop celebrates 33 years of classic, made from scratch American cooking.
